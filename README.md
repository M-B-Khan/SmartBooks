# What is SmartBooks?

Imagine you run a small business—a shop, a freelance agency, or a consulting firm. Every day, you need to:

* Send invoices to clients *(e.g., "Pay me Rs. 50,000 for this project")*
* Track expenses *(e.g., "I spent Rs. 5,000 on office supplies")*
* Follow up with clients who haven't paid yet
* Wonder, *"Will I have enough money next month?"*

Currently, many small business owners in Pakistan manage these tasks using Excel spreadsheets or paper registers. This approach is often:

* Messy
* Time-consuming
* Error-prone

## The Solution: SmartBooks

**SmartBooks** is a web application that allows business owners to manage their finances digitally from a single platform.

With SmartBooks, users can:

* Create and send invoices
* Record and categorize expenses
* Track pending and completed payments
* Monitor cash flow and business finances
* Gain AI-powered insights to better understand their financial health and make smarter business decisions

Instead of spending hours managing spreadsheets or paperwork, SmartBooks simplifies bookkeeping and helps small businesses stay organized and financially informed.

## A Day in the Life of a SmartBooks User

**Bilal runs a small software agency. He uses SmartBooks.**

* 🧾 He creates an invoice for his client → SmartBooks generates a **professional PDF** and emails it automatically
* 💸 He adds an expense: "Bought a laptop charger" → **AI automatically categorizes** it as "Equipment" without him selecting anything
* 📊 He opens the dashboard → Sees he has **Rs. 3 overdue invoices** worth Rs. 120,000 — SmartBooks already sent **reminder emails** to those clients
* 🤖 He asks the AI: "How is my business doing this month?" → Gets a plain English summary: "Your income is up 20% but your expenses on utilities doubled"
* 📈 He checks the **Cash Flow Forecast** → AI predicts he'll be short Rs. 40,000 next month, so he plans ahead
